Scouting

Troop 1784

 

I am in Scout Troop 1784, in Versailles, Kentucky. The troop does at least 1 'outing' every month. We also go to a camp called: Camp Friedlander, its prononced Freedlander. I'm a Second Class scout about to be First Class. I have many merit badges such as: Camping, Personal Fitness, Chess, Shotgun Shooting, and First Aid, and many more! The troop "base" is at Pisgah Church on Pisgah Pike in Versailles. It's in the big white house at the end of the drive, we meet on Monday's at 7pm, unless Woodford County schools are out. Thanks For Reading! Please Join Our Troop!
